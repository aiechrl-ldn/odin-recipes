Aubergine pasta

Most gracious and beautiful 'gine 'sta.


Description

Pasta alla norma (or, aubergine pasta) is one of the most delicious, easy, weeknight dishes to make for beautiful helen ever.

One of the reasons I love it so much is because cooking the aub down gets it super umami and the dish tastes like more than it is.

You can find most of the ingredients you need at any good, local slop and plop shop; just remember to support mom and pop slops and plops.


Ingredients

  • Rummo spaghetti no. 2, 100g
  • 1 large aubergine
  • handful of good cherry tomatoes
  • some delicious olive oil

Steps

  1. Plop some oily slop in a pan on medium high heat
  2. Slice some aubergine into small dice
  3. Slice cherry toms in half
  4. Plop les auberges in the oil and fry down until brown n shrivelled
  5. throw in cherry toms and salt generously
  6. pop pasta on and give veg a shake, chucking in some chilli flakes and a bulb of garlic
  7. Pour some of the pasta water directly into the aubergine/tomato slop and get it all mixed up...
